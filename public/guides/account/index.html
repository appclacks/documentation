Managing your Appclacks account

You’ll need to install the Appclacks CLI in order to perform the actions described in this section.

Change your password

To change your password, execute the appclacks account password change CLI command.

It will ask you for your account email, current password, and new password.

Reset your password

Follow the procedure in the reset password page to reset your password.
